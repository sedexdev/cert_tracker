Certification Tracking Space

About

This is a simple app that will allow me to list and track certifications I want to study. Certification pages will allow me to have quick links to course content and materials, monitor my progress and flash card creation. I can also include exam dates and send email reminders to keep myself on track. Features of this app will include:

  • Separate certification pages
  • Course links
  • Supporting documentation links
  • Articles and updates
  • Timelines
  • Physical flash card creation tracker
  • Email alerts
  • Exam dates

The hope is that by being organised and having a proper view into what I am studying and how much progress I have made will force me to complete the study material, and hopefully earn a few more certs while I prepare to move into DevOps.
